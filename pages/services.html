Courses

The following course's will provide practical strategies and insights which allow for game improvement.

The Pawn

Pawn Power: In this course, you will learn everything you need to know about the most basic chess piece - the pawn. From pawn structures to pawn breaks, we'll cover all the important concepts you need to understand to master this essential piece.

The Rook

Rook Rampage: The rook is a dominant piece that can control entire ranks and files. In this course, we'll focus on the rook's power in open positions, and show you how to use it to launch unstoppable attacks.

The Knight

Horsing Around: The knight is often called the trickiest piece on the board, and for good reason. In this course, we'll explore the unique moves and strategic possibilities of the knight, from tricky forks to sneaky attacks.

The Bishop

Bishop Bonanza: The bishop is a versatile piece that can control long diagonals and launch deadly attacks. In this course, we'll focus on the different ways to use the bishop in your games, from pinning pieces to controlling key squares on the board.

The Queen

Queen's Gambit (the movie): The queen is the most powerful piece on the board, and in this course, you'll learn how to use her to dominate your opponent. From using the queen to control the center to creating unstoppable threats, we'll cover everything you need to know about this essential piece.

The King

King Safety and Endgame Mastery: Protecting your king is crucial in chess, but it's especially important in the endgame when the king can become a powerful attacking piece. In this course, we'll explore various defensive strategies to keep your king safe from harm, and also show you how to use your king to gain an advantage in the endgame.

The Basics

Chess Basics: If you're new to chess, this course is the perfect place to start. We'll cover all the essential rules, from how the pieces move to basic strategies and tactics. By the end of the course, you'll have a solid understanding of the fundamentals of chess.

En Passant

En Passant: En Passant is a unique pawn capture that can catch many beginners off guard. In this course, we'll explore the rules and tactics surrounding this tricky move, and show you how to use it to gain an advantage over your opponent.

Backrank Mate

Backrank Mate: The backrank mate is a powerful tactic that can lead to a quick victory. In this course, we'll explore the different ways to set up and execute this deadly attack, and show you how to avoid falling victim to it yourself.
