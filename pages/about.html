About Squared


My name is Austyn Rowan and I created Squared so people like me could learn more about chess. I am 18 years old. I am a senior in high school and will be graduating in May 2023. I have many hobbies, but chess is one of my favorites. I love to spend my free time playing chess. I hope that with this website I will be able to introduce the game of chess to more people and teach them basic knowledge that they need to know if they want to start playing.



I started to play chess in august of 2021 and introduced my friends to it as well. Initially I had lost a lot of rating points playing against my friends because we did not know how to play, but as I continued to play, I got better. It was not until this last year that my rating started to go up.
